## 👋 About Me

Hi, I'm **Michael Kaburu**, a **Business Technology Transformation Expert, Tax Technology Specialist, Economist, Data Analyst, AI Solutions Developer, and Full-Stack Developer** based in **Nairobi, Kenya**.

I help businesses understand their operational problems, document their processes, identify gaps, and design practical technology-enabled solutions that improve efficiency, reduce manual effort, cut hidden costs, and support better decision-making.

My work sits at the intersection of:

- 🤖 **AI adoption and AI-powered development**
- 🧾 **Tax technology and compliance automation**
- 📊 **Business analytics, reporting, and intelligence**
- 🔁 **Process documentation, process redesign, and automation**
- 💻 **Software development for business transformation**
- ⚙️ **Workflow optimization, reconciliation, and data quality**
- 📝 **Business proposal writing and solution documentation**
- 🎨 **Clean UI/UX and user-friendly digital products**

I focus on turning business challenges into practical, scalable, and measurable digital solutions.
